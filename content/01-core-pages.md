### H2: Frequently Asked Questions — Contact & Booking

**Q1: How do I book an appointment at Yashvini Skin & Hair Clinic?**
You can book by calling us, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the online appointment request form on this page. We will confirm your slot promptly.

**Q2: What should I bring to my first appointment?**
Please bring any relevant medical history, previous prescription slips (if any), and a list of any medications you are currently taking. If you have had previous skin tests or blood tests related to your condition, bring those reports too.

**Q3: How long in advance should I book an appointment?**
For a general dermatology consultation, we typically have appointments available within a few days. For specific procedures, you may need to book 1–2 weeks in advance. Contact us to check current availability.

**Q4: Can I book an appointment for someone else (my child / elderly parent)?**
Yes. You can book on behalf of a family member. Please provide their name and age when booking, especially for paediatric consultations.

**Q5: Is there parking available near the clinic?**
Street parking is available near the clinic. Please call ahead if you have specific accessibility requirements.

**Q6: What should I do if I need to cancel or reschedule?**
Please inform us at least 24 hours before your appointment if you need to cancel or reschedule. This allows us to offer the slot to another patient. You can call or WhatsApp us to make changes.

**Q7: Do you offer emergency or same-day consultations?**
In case of urgent skin concerns (severe allergic reactions, widespread rashes, etc.), please call us directly. We will do our best to accommodate you on the same day where possible.

**Q8: Is there a consultation fee even if I just want to ask a few questions?**
A consultation fee applies to all in-person appointments as this ensures the doctor has dedicated time to properly assess your concern. We believe a proper consultation — even for a "quick question" — is always in the patient's best interest.
